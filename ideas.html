Scope Orrery

Scope Orrery is a virtual solar system that displays amazing planets, moons, and comets. The idea was inspired by the Old Orrery created in 1704. Although it was not very efficient, it inspired us to reinvent it for modern use.

Scope Solar Menu

The Scope Orrery is an incredible experience that needs a powerful, efficient interface, which is where the Solar Menu comes in. This menu contains all the solar system's planets, allowing you to select and zoom in on any planet to explore its incredible details.

Scope Panel

While exploring the stunning details of the planets in the Orrery, the Scope Panel provides fascinating information about each one, enhancing the learning experience by making you feel like you're studying in space itself.
