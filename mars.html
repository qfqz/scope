    Planet information:
    Mars is known as the "Red Planet" due to its iron-rich surface. It has a thin carbon dioxide atmosphere and temperatures that can drop below -100°C.


    Mars has two small moons, Phobos and Deimos, and its day lasts 24.6 hours
