Team Logo

Our logo features the Scorpius constellation, symbolizing passion, strength, and transformation. This makes it a powerful emblem for brands seeking to convey strength and mystery. Additionally, the first three letters of our team name, "Scope," match those of "Scorpius."

Team Name

Our team name is Scope. In project management, "scope" defines project boundaries and deliverables management. Also, "Scope" suggests precision, focusing on tasks with clarity and direction.
